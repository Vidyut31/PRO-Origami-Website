Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • The chameleons' eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs, so they can see two different objects at the same time. This gives them a full 360-degree field of vision.
  • Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.

Interesting facts about Camel

  • Camels travel in herds and both dromedary and Bactrian camels are social creatures. Groups are composed of as many as 30 individuals including a family unit with one dominant male.
  • When a camel does come across water, it can fill up in a hurry, drinking as much as 26 gallons in 10 minutes
  • Camel's milk is lower in cholesterol and higher in vitamin C and minerals like sodium and potassium compared with the milk of other ruminants
